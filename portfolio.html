Big Data
The purpose of this project was to answer a question using a large set of data. We had two questions answered: "What is the frequency of the first letter of each word in the dictionary?" and "What is the percentage of words within the dictionary with a specific length?" These two questions were answered by using repl.it to produce the graphs faster than Canopy. The first graph used a bar chart to best represent the number of words, and the second graph was a pie chart since we used percentages as our measure.
